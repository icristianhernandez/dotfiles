## CV must include:
- Time zone (GMT/EST)
- Languages with certification (EF SET can be free)
- Phone with international format
- LinkedIn (functional link)
- Portfolio link (NOT embedded in the PDF)
- Order: Experience → Education → Skills -> Volunteering → Awards
- "Habilidades computacionales y blandas, programas relevantes al cargo."


Name format: first name + last name
Language: Must be in English unless applying to Spanish companies

### Do NOT include:
- Photo (discrimination liability for US companies)
- Marital status, full address, cédula
- Religion, sexual orientation/gender (unless explicitly asked in UK)
- Hobbies (unless directly relevant)
- Primary/secondary school if you have university
